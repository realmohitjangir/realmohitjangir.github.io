About Me

The story behind
the engineer

Every machine learning model starts with a question. For me, it began with wondering how computers could see, understand, and learn from the world the way humans do. That curiosity turned into a passion that now shapes everything I build.

As a Computer Science Engineering student, I've spent countless hours not just studying algorithms, but applying them to real problems — from detecting deepfakes to predicting career trajectories. I believe the most powerful technology is that which creates tangible impact.

My approach is simple: start with the problem, understand the data deeply, then choose the right tools. Whether it's a CNN for image classification, transfer learning for efficiency, or an end-to-end ML pipeline for production — I care about building things that actually work.

I'm continuously learning, exploring the frontiers of Computer Vision, Natural Language Processing, and Deep Learning, while honing strong fundamentals in Python, statistics, and data engineering.
